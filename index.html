About Platinum Suppliers

Platinum Suppliers is a professional wedding tent and event setup service provider located in Vijayawada. We focus on delivering well-structured, comfortable, and visually pleasing tent arrangements for weddings and traditional functions.

Our experience in handling wedding events allows us to understand space, crowd flow, and ceremonial requirements. Each setup is planned carefully to ensure safety, comfort, and a neat appearance throughout the event.

We cater to various wedding-related occasions such as engagements, receptions, naming ceremonies, and large family gatherings. Our tents are designed to withstand weather conditions while maintaining a clean and organized look.

Being based in Vijayawada, we serve the city and surrounding areas with reliable service, timely execution, and proper coordination with clients and event planners.
